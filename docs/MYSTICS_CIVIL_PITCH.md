## The Problem We Solve

Construction runs on coordination. Today it leaks through the cracks.

| Where work breaks down | What it costs you |
|---|---|
| Daily progress lives in WhatsApp groups and paper registers | No live earned value; surprises at month-end |
| Bills, deductions and payments live in spreadsheets | TDS, retention, advances mis-applied; ledger never tied out |
| Approvals chase people over email and phone | Variations and bills sit for weeks; cash flow stalls |
| Quality and safety records are kept "for the file" | Audit failures; insurance claims rejected |
| Stores and procurement live in another system entirely | Stock-outs, double-ordering, vendor disputes |

> **Mystics Civil replaces every one of those leaks with a single platform — built for the way Indian construction actually works.**

## A Project, End-to-End, in One Place

> **Day 0** — Admin sets up the org, creates users, defines a "Junior PM" custom role.
> **Week 1** — PM creates *Tower B, Sector 7*; owner approves from the inbox.
> **Week 2** — QS builds the L5 BOQ from the live DSR library; storekeeper raises indents; RFQs go to vendors.
> **Every day** — Site engineer marks attendance (geofence verified), files the DPR; PM approves it next morning; WBS updates instantly.
> **A failed cube test** — auto-opens an NCR; contractor submits CAPA; closed in 48 hours.
> **A scope change** — QS drafts a VO; owner approves; flows into billing automatically.
> **Running bill** — TDS, retention, advance all auto-applied; finance pays via NEFT; ledger posts itself.
> **Week 12** — HR runs payroll: compute → approve → release. Done.
> **Day H** — All approvals resolved, NCRs closed, retentions released on schedule. PM marks the project **Completed**. The record becomes a permanent, auditable history.
